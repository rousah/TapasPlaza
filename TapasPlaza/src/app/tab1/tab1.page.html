About Us

We are Johan & Jeannette and have been running our restaurant since 2013, previously known as 'Sabor de Amor'! We are Dutch but we like to call ourselves 'people of the Earth' because we have roots everywhere and love travelling and culture.

Before settling in Spain with our three children we lived in Thailand, which has given us our love and taste for Thai and Asian dishes. Johan is a professional chef and has run big kitchens in Holland, now he puts together the things he loves the most on a plate to share with everyone. Jeannette is an incredible hostess; friendly and heartwarming - she also has a magical touch in the kitchen and baking cakes and sweets. We would love to welcome you into what we call our Home.
